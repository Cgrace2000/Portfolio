About Columbia

As of 2023 the population is 142,416.

Columbia was first chartered as a town in 1805, and a city in 1854.

Columbia is located in the Midland region of South Carolina.

Columbia is considered urban, however with the surrounding towns and smaller communities it has a suburban feel.

The average income is around $55,653, where as the state average is around $66,818
